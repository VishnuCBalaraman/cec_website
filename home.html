EVENTS

Annual Sports Meet

Ashwa is the college’s biggest and the most vibrant Sports Meet providing a great opportunity for students to unite, rejoice and represent themselves. It is a finest chance to inculcate oneself with oodles of power, self confidence and winning Spirit. The two-day extravagant affair held at Christian College Ground on 20 and 21st of May constituted of more than 50 events of March Past, Track and Field, Tug of War etc. The enthusiasm of the participants were infectious, encouraging and a proof of the institute’s dedication to all-round development of its students.

All Kerala Power & Energy Society Student Colloquium

AKPESSC is disposed towards bringing together Power & Energy Society volunteers from all Student Branches in Kerala to foster technological innovation and its a jaunt to discover new ideas. It provides a platform for technological advancements and also helps to reminisce about the activities that tracked their journey as a volunteer and will also get them inspired to divergent thinking by meeting new people. The technical extravaganza will provide a chance to know more about Green Energy and Clean Energy and helps them in redefining goals to persuade in their field of interest.

Summer Internship Program

Training Program to develop technical awareness in the field of Electronics, Electrical and Computer Science Engineering. Course Contents :- Robotics Kit Assembly – Drone Assembly – PCB Design with software tools – Internet of Things (IoT) – Python Programming for AI – Arduino and other development boards – Solar Grid System Design – Web Development – App Development.

PLACEMENT

Training and Placement Cell

The Training and Placement Cell, (TPC) of the College of Engineering, Chengannur is a body of active students, guided by Staff-in-Charge. Student members are specially selected to carry out the responsible working of the cell. The TPC dedicates itself to help students develop their personalities and establish their careers.
